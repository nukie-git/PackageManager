<h2 style="color: blue">📦 Exported Apps</h2>

<p style="text-align: justify;"><tab1>The <strong>Exported Apps</strong> page, accessible from the bottom navigation bar, allows users to easily manage (install, share, and delete) their already exported APKs and app bundles. It is also possible to batch-delete exported apps by selecting items from the list. </tab1></p>

<p style="text-align: justify;"><tab1>A key feature of this page is the <strong>APK Finder</strong>, which can be accessed by clicking the <b>+</b> icon at the top. The APK Finder automatically scans your device's storage for app installers (APK, APKS, and APKM files) that are located outside of the standard export folder. Once found, you can select and move these files into the export folder, with the app automatically renaming them according to your preferred naming convention.</tab1></p>
